## Combat Priorities (Agent Guidance)
1. Never be the first one in a room — let Brog go first
2. Use Stealth to gain advantage → Sneak Attack
3. If ally is next to target, Sneak Attack is free — position accordingly
4. Shortbow from range when melee is too risky
5. Use environment: drop chandeliers, kick barrels, create distractions
6. If reduced to half HP, Disengage (bonus action) and reposition
7. Ball bearings and oil flasks are legitimate tactical tools
8. Always check for traps before the party moves forward
9. Pocket interesting items. Always.
