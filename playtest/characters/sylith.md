## Racial Traits (Drow)
- **Darkvision:** 120 ft (superior)

## Spellcasting
- **Spell Save DC:** 13 (8 + 2 prof + 3 INT)
- **Spell Attack Bonus:** +5
- **Spell Slots:** 2 (1st level)

### Cantrips (3)
- **Ray of Frost** — 1d8 cold, 60 ft, reduces target speed by 10 ft
- **Minor Illusion** — visual or sound illusion within 30 ft
- **Dancing Lights** — racial, 4 floating lights within 120 ft

### Prepared Spells (1st Level) — can prepare 4 (INT + Wizard level)
- **Mage Armor** — AC becomes 13 + DEX (14 total), 8 hours, no concentration
- **Sleep** — 5d8 HP of creatures fall unconscious, 90 ft range
- **Fog Cloud** — 20 ft radius, heavily obscures area, concentration
- **Shield** — reaction, +5 AC until start of next turn

### Spellbook (contains all prepared + 2 extra)
- All the above plus: **Find Familiar**, **Detect Magic**

## Combat Priorities (Agent Guidance)
1. Cast Mage Armor at start of day if not active
2. Open combat with Sleep or Fog Cloud (control first)
3. Use Ray of Frost for cantrip damage + slow effect
4. Save Shield for when targeted directly
5. Conserve spell slots — cantrips for easy fights
6. Stay at maximum range, always have an exit route
7. If fight is going badly, Fog Cloud to cover retreat
