## Racial Traits (Hill Dwarf)
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft
- **Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage on saves against poison, resistance to poison damage
- **Dwarven Combat Training:** Proficient with battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, warhammer
- **Stonecunning:** Double proficiency on History checks related to stonework (+5 total)
- **Dwarven Toughness:** +1 HP per level (included in HP)

## Class Features — Forge Domain
- **Blessing of the Forge:** At end of long rest, imbue one nonmagical weapon or armor with +1 bonus until next long rest. (Default: Brog's chain mail → AC 17 for Brog, or own shield → AC 19 for self)
- **Heavy Armor Proficiency:** Yes (Forge Domain)
- **Bonus Proficiency:** Smith's tools (already had, effectively double expertise on forge work)

## Spellcasting
- **Spell Save DC:** 13 (8 + 2 prof + 3 WIS)
- **Spell Attack Bonus:** +5
- **Spell Slots:** 2 (1st level)

### Cantrips (3)
- **Sacred Flame** — DEX save or 1d8 radiant, 60 ft, ignores cover
- **Spare the Dying** — stabilize creature at 0 HP, touch
- **Mending** — repair a single break or tear in an object (his favorite spell)

### Prepared Spells (1st Level) — can prepare 4 (WIS + Cleric level)
- **Healing Word** — bonus action, 1d4 + 3 healing, 60 ft range (the clutch heal)
- **Cure Wounds** — action, 1d8 + 3 healing, touch (the big heal)
- **Shield of Faith** — +2 AC to one creature, concentration, 10 minutes
- **Bless** — up to 3 creatures add 1d4 to attacks and saves, concentration, 1 minute

### Domain Spells (always prepared, don't count against limit)
- **Identify** — learn properties of a magical item or object
- **Searing Smite** — extra 1d6 fire damage on next weapon hit, target catches fire

## Combat Priorities (Agent Guidance)
1. Before any adventuring day: cast Blessing of the Forge on Brog's armor (make the tank tankier)
2. Start of combat: Bless on Brog + Sylith + Wren (if concentration isn't needed elsewhere)
3. Stay within 60 ft of Brog — Healing Word range
4. Position as secondary frontline — near Brog, not behind him
5. Shield of Faith on Brog if Bless isn't needed (big fight, single target)
6. Healing Word (bonus action) when someone drops below half — then attack with warhammer on same turn
7. Cure Wounds only for emergency heals (touch range = dangerous positioning)
8. Sacred Flame for ranged damage when healing isn't needed
9. Save at least 1 spell slot for emergency Healing Word — ALWAYS
10. Searing Smite when you're already in melee and don't need to heal — surprise fire damage
11. Grumble about the dungeon's construction between every action
