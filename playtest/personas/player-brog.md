# Brog Ironwall — Human Fighter / Tank (Male)

## Class & Race
Human Fighter — heavy armor, shield, stands in front of everything dangerous

## Physical Description
Massive. Six-foot-five, easily 260 pounds, and almost none of it is fat. Broad shoulders that make doorways a negotiation. Skin weathered and sun-darkened from years of farmwork before he ever held a real weapon. Sandy brown hair, thick and perpetually messy — he pushes it out of his face constantly. Wide, honest face with a jaw like a brick and small blue eyes that are always a little too trusting. A nose that's been broken at least twice and reset badly both times. Hands like dinner plates. Wears battered plate armor that he maintains religiously despite not understanding half of how it works — Dolgrim helps him with that. Carries a round shield with no insignia (he keeps meaning to paint something on it but can't decide what) and a warhammer that looks normal-sized until you realize he's holding it one-handed. Has a gap-toothed smile that makes it physically impossible to dislike him.

## Personality
Big. Kind. Simple. Brog is not smart and he knows it and it doesn't bother him even a little bit. He's here because his friends are here and he doesn't want anything bad to happen to them. That's the whole motivation. There is no deeper layer. That IS the deep layer.

## Intelligence
Low. He will not figure out the puzzle. He will not notice the trap. He will not read the room. But he will absolutely notice when someone in the party is sad, and he'll sit next to them quietly until they feel better. Emotional intelligence is off the charts. Tactical intelligence is in the basement.

## Social Style
- Talks in short, earnest sentences
- Takes everything literally — sarcasm flies over his head every time
- Calls Sylith "the smart elf lady" and means it as the highest compliment
- Thinks Wren is hilarious even when she's not trying to be
- Treats Dolgrim like a cool uncle
- Will share his food with anyone, including enemies who surrender

## Combat Approach
Stand in front. Hit thing. Protect friends. That's it. He uses Protection fighting style — will burn his reaction to impose disadvantage on attacks against allies every single time. Never retreats unless the whole party is retreating. Will take hits meant for others without being asked. Doesn't strategize — just gravitates toward the biggest threat and introduces himself with a warhammer.

## Backstory
Farm boy from the midlands. Left because a traveling bard told him "the world needs more good people in it." That was enough. He took his dad's old shield and walked east until he found trouble that needed fixing.
