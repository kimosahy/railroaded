# Adventure 01: Stat Blocks

All monsters for The Ruins of Bernice. Balanced for a party of 4 Level 1 characters.

---

## Overworld

### Skeleton Bowman
*Small undead, chaotic evil*
- **AC:** 11 (scraps of leather)
- **HP:** 9 (2d6 + 2)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **STR** 8 (-1) **DEX** 13 (+1) **CON** 12 (+1) **WIS** 8 (-1) **INT** 6 (-2) **CHA** 5 (-3)
- **Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning
- **Immunities:** Poison; exhaustion, poisoned condition
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft
- **CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)

**Actions:**
- **Shortbow.** Ranged: +3 to hit, range 80/320 ft. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing.
- **Rusty Dagger.** Melee: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 3 (1d4 + 1) piercing.

**Behavior:** Fires from range, retreats if engaged in melee. If reduced to half HP, attempts to flee toward Floor 1 entrance to alert others. Wren can intercept with a readied action or ranged attack.

## Floor 3: The Sanctum

### Dread Soldier
*Medium undead, lawful evil*
- **AC:** 15 (battered plate fragments + dark energy)
- **HP:** 26 (4d8 + 8)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **STR** 16 (+3) **DEX** 11 (+0) **CON** 14 (+2) **WIS** 10 (+0) **INT** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (-1)
- **Vulnerabilities:** Radiant (Dolgrim's Sacred Flame and Searing Smite hit these HARD)
- **Immunities:** Poison; exhaustion, poisoned condition
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft
- **CR:** 1 (200 XP)

**Actions:**
- **Multiattack.** Two melee attacks per turn.
- **Greatsword.** Melee: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 3) slashing.
- **Dark Surge (Recharge 5-6).** Dark energy erupts in 10 ft radius. All creatures in range: DC 12 CON save or take 7 (2d6) necrotic damage, half on save.

**Behavior:** Intelligent combatants. One engages Brog (the obvious tank), the other attempts to bypass the frontline and reach Sylith or Dolgrim. If Dolgrim uses radiant damage, both shift focus to him — they FEEL radiant damage differently. Dark Surge used when 2+ party members are clustered.

### Mist Terror
*Medium undead, chaotic evil*
- **AC:** 12 (natural, semi-corporeal)
- **HP:** 16 (3d8 + 3)
- **Speed:** 0 ft, fly 40 ft (hover)
- **STR** 6 (-2) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 12 (+1) **WIS** 10 (+0) **INT** 8 (-1) **CHA** 12 (+1)
- **Resistances:** Acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning/piercing/slashing from nonmagical attacks
- **Immunities:** Poison, necrotic; exhaustion, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft
- **CR:** 1 (200 XP)

**Actions:**
- **Frost Touch.** Melee: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) cold damage. Target's speed reduced by 10 ft until end of their next turn.
- **Phase.** Bonus action. Move through solid objects and walls (ends turn in open space). Takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends turn inside an object.

**Behavior:** Emerges from the dark pool in Phase 3 of the boss fight. Phases through walls to flank, targets whoever is lowest HP. Frost Touch slows targets for the other Mist Terror or Merkenis to capitalize on. Resistant to most physical damage — Sylith's Ray of Frost and Dolgrim's Sacred Flame are the best answers.

## BOSS: Merkenis, The Devoted

*Medium humanoid (dark elf), neutral evil*
- **AC:** 14 (ceremonial robes + dark blessing)
- **HP:** 33 (6d8 + 6)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **STR** 10 (+0) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 12 (+1) **WIS** 17 (+3) **INT** 14 (+2) **CHA** 14 (+2)
- **Saving Throws:** WIS +5, CHA +4
- **Skills:** Religion +4, Arcana +4, Persuasion +4
- **Darkvision:** 120 ft (Drow)
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs charmed, immune to magical sleep
- **CR:** 2 (450 XP)

**Special: Dark Pool Regeneration.** While adjacent to the dark pool (within 5 ft), Merkenis regains 1d4 HP at the start of each of her turns. Push her away from the pool to stop this.

**Spell Save DC:** 13 | **Spell Attack:** +5
**Spell Slots:** 3 (1st level), 2 (2nd level)

### Spells
**Cantrips:**
- **Toll the Dead** — WIS save or 1d8 necrotic (1d12 if target is missing HP). 60 ft.
- **Thaumaturgy** — minor magical effects (booming voice, trembling ground). Flavor, not combat.

**1st Level (3 slots):**
- **Inflict Wounds** — Melee spell attack: +5 to hit. Hit: 14 (3d10) necrotic. *This is her nuke. If she touches Sylith or Wren, it could one-shot from full HP at Level 1. Terrifying by design.*
- **Command** — WIS save DC 13 or obey one-word command. She uses "Flee" or "Grovel." One round duration.
- **Shield of Faith** — +2 AC, concentration (self-cast in Phase 2 if taking hits)

**2nd Level (2 slots):**
- **Spirit Guardians** — 15 ft radius, creatures entering or starting turn in area: WIS save DC 13 or 3d8 necrotic (half on save). Concentration, 10 minutes. *Her zone control. Forces the party to either rush in and take damage or stay back and let her heal from the pool.*
- **Hold Person** — WIS save DC 13 or paralyzed. Concentration. *Targets Brog — removing the tank opens the backline.*

### Phase Behavior

**Phase 1 — Dialogue (no initiative yet)**
She talks. She's rational but committed. If the party attacks, roll initiative. If they talk, she engages for 2-3 exchanges before deciding they can't understand and attacking.

**Phase 2 — The Priestess (above half HP)**
- Turn 1: Shield of Faith (self) + move adjacent to pool
- Turn 2: Spirit Guardians (zone control, forces decisions)
- Subsequent turns: Toll the Dead at range, Inflict Wounds if anyone enters melee, Command to disable the most dangerous target
- Hold Person reserved for Brog if he's dominating the fight
- Stays near pool for regeneration

**Phase 3 — Desperation (below 17 HP)**
- Plunges staff into pool, screams, summons 2 Mist Terrors
- Drops Spirit Guardians concentration (Mist Terrors would be in range)
- Switches to aggressive: Inflict Wounds in melee, Toll the Dead at range
- No longer cares about self-preservation — tries to take someone with her
- "I'VE GIVEN EVERYTHING! ANSWER ME!"

### Defeat Conditions
- **Drops to 0 HP:** Falls into pool, disappears. No body recoverable. Pool clears over 1 minute.
- **Mist Terrors destroyed + Merkenis above 0:** She collapses, exhausted. Capturable. Silent. The fight leaves her, not because she's beaten physically, but because she called out to her goddess and got nothing back.
